Bubble tea!

Learn all you want to know about bubble tea (aka boba)!

What is bubble tea?

Bubble tea, commonly known as boba, is a sweet drink that consists of a liquid and tapioca pearls. The tapioca pearls are soft and chewy, which adds a new feature of enjoyment when having a drink.

Where did bubble tea come from?

Bubble tea originated in Taiwan (Tainan and Taichung) in the 1980s. Since then, it has spread internationally and is loved by people worldwide.
